How to use Blood Donation System?

Please register using pc or laptop,because it develops primarily for desktops.So, if you use laptop,it is easy to register and explore our application.After that, you can use hand held devices.

1) Registration

If you are a donor, first register in our website.In home page,on the top right side,
you can see the register link i.e,Register(For Donor). Go to that link and provide the
neceessary details and submit the details.After submitting, you will get your password
on registered email id.Prerequisite:1)You must have to be adult(greater than 18).Otherwise, you cannot able to register.

2) Login

After donor registration completed, go to login page and provide the credentials correctly to login. You can also reset password if you either forgetted or deleted the mail (comes when registration completed).

3) Profile Page

When you log in to your account, the details which you provided during registration will be present initially. If you want to update details, you can do it by edit button.In account page,below the details you can see some topics called Progress and Photos Tab.In progress, you can add your past and present donate details and also you can add photos(for each progress details, you can able to add maximum one photo).If you add one detail which happened before the donate details you already added, our website will sort out and display in correct order.This details are extremely important and should be reliable because from this details we take your last blood donated date and do our process.

4) Requsting Donors

Finally, for blood needed people or blood bank can request their donors by providing informations about blood group,district,city,hospital name(not needed for blood bank) and our website process with the informations and provide donors details.Our website also sent email to all that sorted out donors about the needed people or blood bank information.
